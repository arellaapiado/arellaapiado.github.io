meno-pals

Made in COGS 187A

The Problem

Menopause isn't talked about. People, especially women, refuse to talk about private matters like Menopause because it's still taboo.

The Solution

Introducing meno-pals, an interactive dashboard app catered to connect women between the ages 40-60, who are about to or are currently going through Menopause. As the app is solely for women, we aspire to promote comfortability, relatability, and understandability as each woman can empathize given their same experiences.

SwimGym

Made in COGS 3

The Problem

Swimming is a fun and great way to exercise. But how do people keep track of their progressions when they're too busy swimming and have no one around?

The Solution

SwimGym is an app that helps people pace themselves while swimming. By connecting their phone to a speaker, SwimGym counts for users while they are doing laps around the pool and helps record each duration of the lap.
